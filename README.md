# Casey's General Stores (caseys-general-stores)
Casey's General Stores is one of the largest convenience store chains in the United States, operating more than 2,900 stores selling fuel, made-from-scratch pizza, prepared food, and other convenience items primarily in small midwestern communities. Casey's is a publicly traded Fortune 500 company (NASDAQ: CASY) and offers a mobile app for pizza ordering, delivery, and its Casey's Rewards loyalty program. No public developer APIs have been documented; partner and supplier integrations are managed privately.
